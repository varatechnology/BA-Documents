### Executive Summary – Step 3: Specific Field-Level Verification

**Step 3** empowers verifiers to conduct precise, targeted validation of particular certificate details (such as marks, subject, or registration number) after a credential has already passed initial and full data integrity checks. This stage is critical for scenarios where verifiers—such as employers, embassies, or institutions—need to match information provided by the candidate with a physical or scanned copy.

**How it Works:**
- The verifier, having obtained candidate consent and completed primary verification, selects specific fields they wish to validate (e.g., grade, exam year).
- Certcheck transmits only the selected field-check request to the issuer’s original records using a secure, privacy-respecting API.
- The issuer’s system confirms whether each field matches the data on file, without exposing any unrelated personal or certificate information.
- The results are returned as a clear "Match" or "Mismatch" for each requested field, supporting side-by-side comparisons if the verifier has a document copy.
- Every verification action is securely logged, and the candidate is notified, ensuring full transparency.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Granular Trust:** Verifiers can validate only what they need—nothing more—promoting data minimization and compliance.
- **Privacy by Design:** No bulk data is exchanged; only field-specific requests and results, reducing risk and exposure.
- **Audit & Transparency:** Every request is logged and visible to the candidate for peace of mind and regulatory accountability.
- **Supports Complex, Real-World Scenarios:** Enables trustworthy, issuer-backed comparison against paper, scanned, or declared records in sensitive processes such as recruitment or immigration.

Step 3 delivers the ultimate degree of confidence and control—making certified verification more precise, privacy-conscious, and verifiable than ever before.
