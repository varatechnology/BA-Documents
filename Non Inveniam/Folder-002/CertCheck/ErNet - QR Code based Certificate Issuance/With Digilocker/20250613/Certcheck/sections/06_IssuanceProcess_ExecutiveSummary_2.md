### Executive Summary (For Non-Technical Stakeholders)

The **Certcheck Platform** brings a new level of security and trust to digital certificates for students and professionals. We work together with DigiLocker to make sure every certificate is genuine, instantly verifiable, and safely delivered to the rightful owner.

**How it Works:**  
- **Easy Issuance:** Schools and other institutions create and sign certificates using their own secure systems.
- **No Data Risks:** Your personal certificate data stays with your school and yourself. We never store your details on our platform.
- **Lasting Proof:** We use blockchain technology to record a permanent proof that your certificate is real and unaltered.
- **Instant Access:** You receive your certificate in DigiLocker—your private, government-backed digital locker—and a simple QR code for easy sharing.
- **Quick Verification:** Anyone can scan the QR code to instantly check that your certificate exists, is unedited, and really belongs to you.

**Benefits:**  
- **Safe from Fraud:** Certificates can’t be faked or tampered with.
- **Full Privacy:** Your personal information is never exposed or stored with us.
- **Trusted Everywhere:** Government, employers, and foreign authorities can all recognize and accept your credential with confidence.

**Certcheck** gives you fast, safe, and trusted certificates—ready to use for jobs, travel, or further studies.
